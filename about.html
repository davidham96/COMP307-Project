About Us

Hi! We are a team of 4 McGill computer science undergraduate students! We built this project to make our campus a more sustainable place. Read more about us and our project below:

The project

Our website can drive awareness and engagement in campus sustainability efforts, making it easier for students to adopt eco-friendly habits. An interactive website that puts sustainability on the map. Sustainability resources often go underutilized because students and staff don't know they exist. Water refill stations, recycling bins, and bike racks might be scattered across campus, but without clear visibility, their impact is limited. Additionally, the absence of a centralized platform for eco-events and initiatives hinders engagement. Our platform helps users identify eco-friendly resources on campus such as water refill stations, bike racks, green spaces, recycling bins or sustainability events happening in or around campus.
